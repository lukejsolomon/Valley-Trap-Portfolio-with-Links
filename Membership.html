Family Membership

We have 2 types of memberships at the trap range.

1- Working membership - You write two $50 checks. One for your membership and one as a $50 deposit check. You will get your deposit check back when you work one shoot. If you do not work a shoot then the second check will be cashed.

2- Non-working membership. $100 no volunteering hours needed.

When you become a member you will have access to our token machine. The token machine is so that you can come out and shoot trap anytime you want. But you will need to have at least one other person with you to push the button and for safety reasons.

Your practices will be $3 and the tokens are also $3.

For more information email ValleyTrapRange@aol.com Or call 402-721-8465.
